After the reverse run of 'MANIAC', VIVIZ's fifth mini-album 'VOYAGE', which is being released in about a year, is an album that encapsulates VIVIZ's journey to become the master of the life they have pioneered themselves and will continue to do so in the word 'travel'. They create their own world through the act of freely traveling through an imperfect life and building their own complete life. They see you through 'me' and welcome you into their world. Maybe it could be traveling through VIVIZ's world.
